**What are you building? Who is it for?:** I’m building a website that helps users manage their grocery shopping by creating a dynamic list of items they already have and what they still need to buy. The platform allows you to easily add and remove items, keeping everything organized in one place. In addition, it suggests recipes based on the ingredients you already have, making it easier to plan meals, reduce food waste, and get more value out of your groceries. I am building this for me and my roommates, to maxamize space in our shared fridge.  

**Why? What motivates you to build this? What problem does it solve?:** I’m building this website for me and my roommates because we share a small refrigerator between six girls, which makes it hard to keep track of what we have and what we need. The goal is to simplify grocery shopping and make planning dinners easier by keeping a shared, organized list of ingredients, sending reminders for items we’re running low on, and helping us make better use of the food we already have.

**MVP vs. Stretch Goals: What is the minimum working version? What would you add if you have time?:** The minimum version is a website that you can add grocery items into a list. If I had time I would add maybe experation dates to set reminders if an item is going to go bad soon or when you're running low. I would also add a way to orangize the food into categories. 


**What technologies do you plan to use? APIs, libraries, frameworks, etc.:** I am planning to use an AI to ask for meal ideas based off what is in the pantry.
